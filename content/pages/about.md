Hello, I'm Stephen Malone and this is Gesedels, a journal of haiku about the melancholy minutia in our daily lives. I've admired the art of haiku for a long time, and I'm also inspired by music like [Dry Cleaning][1] and [Gang of Youths][2] that highlights the smallest moments of our lives. This blog is my attempt to combine the two.

I post every Monday, Wednesday and Friday (Australian time). If you want to keep up, you can subcribe to the [RSS feed][3] or follow me on Bluesky at [@gesedels][4]. If you'd like to get in touch, you can DM me at [@gedesels][4] or email [mail@gesedels.com][5].

Thank you for reading.
